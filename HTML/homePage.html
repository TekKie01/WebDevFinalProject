ABOUT WPC

"WPC Wants to Help Simplify The Process of Acheiving the Wedding of Your Dreams..." ~ Tess M. (founder of WPC)

Planning a wedding can be stressful. The custom Wedding Planner Checklist application is a "MUST-HAVE" for a bride-to-be that is constantly on the go. The main goal of this application is to help future brides achieve the wedding of their dreams while staying organized, calm, and as stress-free as possible! This virtual wedding planner aims to keep brides on track as they gather the many details of pre-wedding parties, the wedding day, and things to do after the wedding. Check out what the WPC Wedding Planner Checklist can do. Many brides wept in gratitude for WPC's concise fill-in lists and charts & many brides boasted that the WPC Wedding Planner provided the essential reference needed to organize their dream wedding. Enjoy the wedding planning process with your own custom WPC Wedding Planner App by your side. Download, for Free, a generic WPC wedding planner today. It is available on all mobile devices and web browsers.

Welcome to WPC! Brandon and I (Tess) would like for you to get to know us more. Brandon and I got married over a year ago. We understand what it is like planning a wedding. The difficulties we underwent days before our wedding almost made us want to throw-in-the-towel and elope! However,as tempting as elopement was, Brandon and I could never leave our families out of one of the biggest days of our lives. Many times we were criticized for not hiring a wedding planner. My reply to that was always, "Yes, a wedding planner would have been nice to have... IF WE COULD AFFORD ONE!." Therefore, after our wedding, Brandon and I made a promise to help future couples, like us, plan their wedding. Marriage is beautiful, and with the proper guidance, planning your dream wedding can and should be enjoyable too! Let WPC help you with your wedding planning because when you are happy, Brandon and I are happy! ~ Tess M.
